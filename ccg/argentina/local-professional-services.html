U.S. consulting firms with local subsidiaries, as well as major local players, provide a wide scope of business solutions that include IT consulting, tax work, and M&A due-diligence, and market research. The U.S. Commercial Service prepares Industry Sector market research reports on an ongoing basis.

Customized Market Research (CMR) is also available for companies that wish to have specific questions answered such as: the overall marketability of a product or service; market trends and size, customary distribution and promotion practices; market entry requirements; regulations; product standards and registration; key competitors, and potential agents, distributors, or strategic partners.
